Welcome to Sublimity!
We are redifining mental health outreach through action, education, and integration.
Sublimity provides users with the power to take control of their mental health by providing them access to research about various mental health disorders, coping mechanisms for various mental health disorders, information about the closest mental health clinics, the ability to connect with mental health professionals,
and a user-friendly chatbot which allows users to jot down their emotions freely!
